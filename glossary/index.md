# Governance Glossary

The Hollow House Institute Governance Glossary defines key concepts used to analyze governance structures, authority systems, and behavioral dynamics within organizations and AI-integrated environments.

---

## Core Governance Concepts

### Behavioral Drift
Gradual deviation between intended policy and actual operational behavior.

### Governance Debt
Accumulated governance risk created by postponed or unresolved governance decisions.

### Escalation Decay
The loss of effectiveness in escalation systems caused by repeated unresolved alerts.

### Stop Authority
The formal authority to halt processes when safety or governance risk thresholds are exceeded.

---

These concepts form part of the Hollow House Institute governance framework for analyzing institutional risk and organizational behavior.
